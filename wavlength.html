Wavlength

Project Overview

Project management software built for creatives. Designers share profile anywhere on the internet to produce lead generation and conversion.

Technologies Used

Git JIRA Confluence

Detailed Description

Wavlength is a customer relationship management (CRM) and productivity platform designed to empower freelance designers by streamlining lead generation and project management processes. This platform enables designers to showcase their portfolios and service offerings through customizable online profiles that can be easily shared across various digital channels, including social media and custom links.

Wavlength primarily focuses on entry-level freelance designers who possess the creative skills to succeed commercially but may lack the business acumen necessary to scale their operations. Many freelancers struggle to maintain a successful business due to the time-consuming nature of essential tasks such as marketing, negotiation, and project management.

With the freelance workforce projected to exceed 50% of the total workforce by 2025, there is a growing need for tools and platforms that empower freelancers to succeed in their ventures. Wavlength aims to bridge this gap by providing a comprehensive solution that addresses the unique challenges faced by freelance designers.

Key Features

  • Lead Generation
    • Potential clients can view a designer's profile, which highlights their portfolio and the plans or services they offer. Clients can then submit detailed project proposals, specifying their requirements, budget, and desired timeline.
  • Proposal Management
    • Designers can access a dedicated portal to review and manage incoming project proposals. They have the option to accept or decline proposals based on their availability and interest in the project.
  • Secure Payments
    • Once a designer accepts a proposal, the client verifies the project details and makes an upfront payment, ensuring financial security for both parties.
  • Project Management
    • After payment, the client is redirected to a project portal where they can track the progress of their project in real-time and communicate directly with the designer..

Future Improvements

Wavlength is no longer in production. If the team were to enhance the platform, we would leverage crypto payments to enhance the transaction experience. Majority of the designers on the platform were from various parts of the world and one of the biggest issues that we encountered was getting access to a global payment processor that would accommodate all of our designers.
